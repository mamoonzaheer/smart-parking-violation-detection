About ParkSafe AI

ParkSafe AI is an innovative parking management system designed to tackle the challenges of urban parking violations. Using advanced computer vision and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology, it can detect improperly parked vehicles and instantly notify the management team.

By leveraging artificial intelligence, ParkSafe AI provides real-time monitoring, detailed violation reports, and instant alerts, ensuring a smooth and systematic approach to parking management.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI detect parking violations?

Our system uses advanced computer vision and OCR technology to recognize violations in real-time, analyzing vehicle positions and parking regulations.

What types of violations can the system detect?

The system can detect various violations including double parking, parking in no-parking zones, expired parking permits, and unauthorized parking.

How accurate is the system?

Our AI system boasts a 99% accuracy rate in violation detection, with continuous learning and improvement capabilities.

Can the system be integrated with existing security systems?

Yes, ParkSafe AI is designed to seamlessly integrate with existing security and parking management systems.
